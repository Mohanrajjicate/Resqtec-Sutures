Our Story

Founded in 2015, Resqtec Sutures Pvt Ltd was born from a vision to provide Indian and global healthcare markets with surgical sutures that meet the highest international standards of quality and affordability. Our journey began with a dedicated team of biomedical engineers and surgeons committed to closing the gap between excellence and accessibility in surgical care.

Our Mission

To empower surgeons and enhance patient outcomes by manufacturing and supplying a comprehensive range of technologically advanced, reliable, and sterile surgical sutures.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized and trusted name in the field of wound closure technology, known for our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Where Precision is Engineered

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, located in the MedTech Park of Chennai, is the heart of our operations. It is equipped with automated machinery, stringent environmental controls, and Class 10,000 cleanrooms to ensure every suture is produced under the most sterile conditions. Our quality assurance protocols are integrated into every step of the manufacturing process, from raw material inspection to final product packaging and sterilization.
